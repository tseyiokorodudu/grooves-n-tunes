My Playlists, Your Ears

Harnessing music's ability to affect emotions, heal hearts and bring people together

I'm passionate about music. It has always been a way for me to relax, express myself, access my emotions and connect with others. I love finding new music that I can share with people, and I created this website to do just that. I also share my own stories and experiences with music, so you can get to know me better and learn more about my passion. I hope you find something you love on my website.
